1. Open the "usb30_hub_fw_updater.app".
1. The current PID and VID will be displayed on the UI (User Interface).
1. Modify the PID and VID.
1. Choose the specific hub bin file.
1. Update  FW
1. After replugging the device, the modified PID and VID will be displayed.
