### Debug log archive
### Grab the log process
1. Unzip DebugFolder.zip
1. Open Command Prompt with Administrator privileges
1. Execute getLog.bat
1. Systeminfo.txt & usbViewlog.txt will be generated in the DebugFolder folder, please send these two log files back to us for analysis
